Formats

Standard

Ever rotating format. Only allows the most recent cards (printed in the last 2 years).

Modern

Much larger format. Contains cards dating back to 2003. So any card that has been printed since then is allowed.

Commander

One of the most popular formats. It allows any card that has ever been printed, over 20,000 cards to choose from. There is a modest list of cards not allowed

The Colors of Magic: The Gathering (WUBRG)

  • White (W)
  • Blue (U)
  • Black (B)
  • Red (R)
  • Green (G)
  • White stands for law, structure, and equality. This color is for those who want to protect themselves and leverage small effective creatures.
  • Blue stands for knowledge, deceit, and control. This color is for those who want to control the game and the way their opponent plays the game.
  • Black stands for power, death and sacrifice. This color is for those who want to win no matter what it takes. They are willing to sacrifice their own life and creatures to win the game.
  • Red stands for aggression, impulse and destruction. This color is for those who want to come out swinging with aggressive cards and keep their opponent on the back foot.
  • Green stands for nature, wildlife, and growth. This color is for those who want to play big splashy creatures and overrun their oppnent with them.
